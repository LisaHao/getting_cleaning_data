This codebook summarizes data fields in tiny_data.txt.

The tiny_data.txt file has 2 identifiers:
- subject: the ID of subject in study
- activity: the activity they were doing at the time when the measurements were taken, which include walking, walking upstairs, walking downstairs, sitting, standing and laying.
